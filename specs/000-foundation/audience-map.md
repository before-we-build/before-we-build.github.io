# Audience map — public site

Status: public-safe foundation note.

The public site serves an ordinary Christian visitor who wants to pause before a serious decision and examine the foundation before God.

## Primary public visitor

A Christian considering a serious next step:

- relationship or marriage direction;
- family decision;
- ministry responsibility;
- shared work;
- another decision where building without counsel would be unwise.

## Public promise

The site offers a private preparation path, not an answer about the person or the future.

## Primary action

Go to Start Alone, choose a Scripture passage, answer short questions, and decide what should be brought to prayer, Scripture, and wise counsel.

## Secondary readers

Pastors, mentors, family members, and mature believers may use the site as a set of questions for conversation, but not as authority over a person's decision.
